Myers-Briggs Test:

The myers-briggs test revealed that 3 out of the 4 of us are more introverted than extroverted and are more prone to examine and observe others' behaviours. This information is helpful because it allows us as a group to understand that we need to speak up more to communicate better and improve workflow. All of the team members think logically and don't react impulsively therefore we can come to conclusions with ease and little to no arguments.

Learning Style Test:

Three of our team members are visual learners and one is an auditory learner, knowing this allows us as a team to understand how each other learns and how to go about delegating roles and instructing each other. For example in our meetings we know to provide visuals for those of us who are visual learners (Sharing screens showing assignment sheets, current work etc.)

Third Learning Test of Choice:

These different tests were hard to compare and contrast with each other as each team member completed a different test. However the results of these various tests only reinforced the outcomes that the last two tests provided, this being that most of our group are logical introverted thinkers with one team member being an extroverted logical observer.
